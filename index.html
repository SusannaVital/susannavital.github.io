Basic Info About Me

When I was a child, I had a lovely kid’s toy sewing machine. Yes, I was one of those kids that prefer to design their doll’s clothes because stores simply cannot get it right. One beautiful day, my parents found themselves surprised to discover me disassembling my sewing machine just to discover how it worked. As one might guess, this was an omen that I would follow the path to became an engineer. During my high school, I got my Mechanical Technician Diploma, and later on I became an Electronic Engineer. Recently, I have finished my Master’s program in Electromagnetics and Microwaves. During my academic years, I was able to design, assemble and program different robots, circuit boards and follow my curiosity into different projects. For those of you who are wondering, I now own an adult sewing machine and love to get creative during my free time.
