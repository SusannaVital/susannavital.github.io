Info

This charming line following robot was built ang programmed from scratch by myself and one more person. It was programmed entirely in Assembly (because we love a good chalenge) with the following functionalities: follow a line, sense obstacles and turn on/off with claps.
We chose the require sensors to perform the desired actions. Then we bought the this toy truck at a dollar store and added the motors to it, which was a quite fun part of the project.
